JUPEB Guide 2026: Registration, Fees, and Admissions

Everything you need to know about JUPEB 2026 and how BIA JUPEB helps you secure 200 Level admission.

What is JUPEB?

JUPEB (Joint Universities Preliminary Examinations Board) is a one-year A-Level program recognized by Nigerian universities. Successful candidates gain Direct Entry admission into 200 Level without JAMB. BIA JUPEB prepares students with focused classes, mock exams, and counseling to maximize admission outcomes in 2026.

JUPEB 2026 Fees (Form and Programme)

The official JUPEB registration form fee starts from N15,000, while BIA JUPEB programme fees cover lectures, learning materials, and student support. For full fee breakdowns, see the JUPEB programme details page.

How JUPEB Points Are Calculated

JUPEB grades convert to points as follows:

  • A = 5 points
  • B = 4 points
  • C = 3 points
  • D = 2 points
  • E = 1 point
