JUPEB vs IJMB: Which Is Easier in 2026?

A clear comparison to help you choose the right A-Level program.

Duration

JUPEB typically runs for about 8 to 9 months, while IJMB can take 9 to 12 months depending on the study center. If you want a faster route to Direct Entry in 2026, JUPEB is often preferred.

University Acceptance

Both programs are accepted by many Nigerian universities. BIA JUPEB focuses on placement opportunities into University of Lagos (UNILAG) for the 2026 session.

Subject Combination and Exam Style

JUPEB uses a modular exam style with a clear points system (A=5, B=4, C=3, D=2, E=1). IJMB uses a similar grading system but may differ in academic calendar and exam format. Your success depends on quality preparation and consistency.

Which Is Easier?

"Easier" depends on your study habits and support system. JUPEB is a shorter program, which many students prefer because it reduces time and cost. IJMB is longer but may give extra time for deeper revision. If you want a focused and structured approach, JUPEB with BIA JUPEB is an excellent choice.
