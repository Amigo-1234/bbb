How to Gain Admission into UNILAG via JUPEB 2026

Follow these steps to secure 200 Level admission into the University of Lagos with JUPEB.

Step 1: Register for JUPEB 2026 with BIA JUPEB

Start by completing your registration for the 2026 JUPEB session. BIA JUPEB provides guidance on documentation, payment, and class schedules. Register here: JUPEB registration page.

Step 2: Choose the Right Subject Combination

UNILAG considers your JUPEB subjects for admission. Your combination must match your intended course. For example:

  • Medicine: Biology, Chemistry, Physics
  • Law: Literature-in-English, Government, CRS
  • Engineering: Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry

Step 3: Aim for High JUPEB Points

UNILAG is competitive. The higher your JUPEB points, the better your chances. Points are calculated as A=5, B=4, C=3, D=2, E=1. BIA JUPEB provides mock exams and weekly tests to help you achieve top results.

Step 4: Apply for UNILAG Direct Entry

After completing JUPEB, apply for UNILAG Direct Entry within the approved window. BIA JUPEB will guide you on timelines and required documentation.

Step 5: Follow Up and Prepare

Keep all your JUPEB results and admission documents ready. Continue academic preparation, as UNILAG may request additional screening or verification.

Need help? Visit the JUPEB Guide 2026 or contact BIA JUPEB for personalized assistance.
