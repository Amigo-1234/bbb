Can I Use Awaiting Result for JUPEB 2026?

Yes, you can register with awaiting result, but there are important steps to follow.

Short Answer: Yes

Most JUPEB study centers accept candidates with awaiting WAEC, NECO, or NABTEB results. However, you must complete your O-Level requirements before the final JUPEB registration closes. BIA JUPEB will guide you through the timeline for 2026.

What Documents You Need

  • Recent passport photograph
  • Valid ID (if available)
  • O-Level result or evidence of registration for awaiting result

When to Upload Your Result

Once your results are out, submit them immediately to avoid delays in final registration. This is essential for Direct Entry admission into 200 Level.

Tips to Avoid Problems

Register early, attend classes consistently, and keep communication open with the BIA JUPEB team. Early registration improves your chances of securing a study slot.

For more details, see the JUPEB Guide 2026 or start your registration.
